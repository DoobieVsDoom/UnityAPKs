DoobieVsDoom
EVOL AYRR 

Android 7 (API level 24) and above.
Download, Install and Run.
Basic Augmented Reality test for Mobile development.
Requires a Marker to initiate (marker-based augmented reality).
Marker of Marilyn Munroe used to initiate appearance of Character.
Marker is included in repository.
Swipe left, right, up, down for various character movements.

Once downloaded and installed,
direct camera at Marilyn Munroe marker on screen.
Character will appear and user can swipe for character movement.
